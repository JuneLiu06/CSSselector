Discover a variety of snowboarding tricks, from basic maneuvers to advanced aerial grabs. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, there's always something new to learn!

Freestyle Snowboarding Tricks

Ollie

The foundational trick where riders jump off the tail of their board. Essential for many advanced tricks.

Nollie

Similar to an Ollie, but executed by springing off the nose of the board instead of the tail.

Nose Grab

The rider grabs the nose of the snowboard with their leading hand while in the air.

Tail Grab

The rider grabs the tail of the snowboard with their trailing hand while in the air.

Indy Grab

The rider grabs the middle of the snowboard with their trailing hand while in the air.

Method Grab

A stylish trick where the rider grabs the heel edge with their leading hand, kicks out their rear foot, and arches their back.
